Quiz India

Welcome to Incredible India Quiz

This Quiz is just for fun and let you discover how much you know about a beautiful, historical rich, culturally diverse country called INDIA.

This game have 3 levels 'Easy', 'Medium' and 'Hard'. Each level consist of 5 unique questions about India. When you give correct answer of a question it will help you to score 10 points. Whereas for every incorrect answer you will loose 5 points.

By default, the game will start from 'Easy' level. But you have option to choose either 'Medium' or 'Hard' difficulty levels as well. There is no limit how many times you can restart this game.

Please Enter you name, select difficulty level below and click on 'Start Quiz' button to start the game. Good luck :)
